📒 Yazboz — Smart Digital Score Keeper

Stop carrying pen and paper for Okey, Pişti and other card games! Yazboz brings all the benefits of the classic yazboz notebook to your phone with smart automation.

🎯 FEATURES

📊 Colored Classic Okey Mode
• Automatic multiplier based on floor color
• Blue (×3), Red (×4), Black (×5), Yellow (×6)
• Auto-detect winner color from top row
• Manual color selection for closed hands

⭐ Special Finish Support
• "Okey" finish and "Pair-going" finish
• Automatic ×100 / ×2 multipliers
• Winner: color × -100 bonus
• Losers: raw penalty × 2× color

⚠️ Penalty System
• One-tap penalty addition
• Floor color × 100 calculation
• Extra penalty for player mistakes

🎮 Game Modes
• Singles: 4 individual players
• Pairs: 2 team competition

🔄 Round-Based Structure
• Unlimited rounds
• Edit past rounds anytime
• Separate multiplier per round
• Forward/back navigation

👥 Player Names
• Custom player/team names
• Tap to edit
• Drag-to-reorder columns

📜 Game History
• All games saved automatically
• Detailed round-by-round view
• Historical analysis

🌙 Dark / ☀️ Light Mode
• Eye-friendly themes
• Instant switching

🔒 PRIVACY
• No internet required (fully offline)
• No data collection or sharing
• All data stays on your device

Perfect for Pişti, Okey, Renkli Klasik, Düz 101 and similar card games.
